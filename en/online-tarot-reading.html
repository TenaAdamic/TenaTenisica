Preparations Before Tarot Reading

1. What’s bugging you? Start there

Love, money, existential dread — the usual suspects. You’ll see a few topic options. Go with the one that’s currently messing with your head the most.

2. Be real with yourself

Drop the act. Before you click anything, pause. Where are you right now—mentally, emotionally? What’s actually bugging you? What’s the question that keeps you up at night? Go deeper. The cards don’t care for surface-level stuff.

3. Quiet the noise

Find yourself a pocket of peace. This isn’t a “scroll while watching Netflix” situation. Tarot needs space, focus, and just a pinch of silence. It’s not about magic, it’s about showing up and actually being here.

4. You know this is online, right?

Automated online readings often use generic interpretations of the cards. Keep in mind that the messages may be broader and less specific than in a personal reading. Try to adapt them to your situation in a way that makes the most sense to you.

5. Write it down, seriously

When the reading’s done, jot down the cards and what they said to you. Come back to it in a day, a week—whenever. Sometimes the message doesn’t hit until your brain’s had time to marinate in it.

6. See the bigger picture

If one interpretation seems unclear, consider combining messages from multiple cards. Look at the bigger picture and try to connect the different aspects highlighted by the cards.

7. Trust yourself

Your gut beats any generic card description. If something hits you, sparks curiosity, or gives you chills, that’s your real answer. Don’t look for validation, seek resonance.

8. Use it as an additional tool

Online tarot reading can be a useful tool for reflection, but treat it as an addition to other methods of self-development and decision-making. If you feel the need for deeper insight, consider a personal reading.

What Happened, What’s Happening, What’s Next

Ever feel like your life’s got hidden layers? Like your past is low-key haunting your now, and the future’s just sitting there, smirking? This tarot spread doesn't mess around — 6 heavy-hitter cards from the Major Arcana break it all down: where you’re at, how you got here, and what might be coming if you don’t self-sabotage.

It looks simple, sure, but don’t let that fool you. This method’s cracked open minds and hit people with truth they weren’t ready for (but needed). So if you're ready to stop scrolling and actually look inward, the cards are waiting.

The Fool

You are ready for a new adventure! This card invites you to step into the unknown with full confidence. Without fear and with innocence, open yourself to new opportunities that life offers you. If you feel excitement and uncertainty, it may be time to embrace change and explore what awaits you around the corner.

The Magician

You have everything you need to achieve your dreams! This card reminds you that your abilities and resources are key to success. Use your creativity and skills to manifest what you want in your life. Now is the right time to turn your ideas into reality.

The High Priestess

It’s time to retreat into silence and listen to your inner wisdom. This card invites you to connect with your instincts and seek answers within yourself. You may discover hidden truths and deeper insights if you focus on your inner world.

The Empress

Feel the abundance and creative energy that surrounds you! This card brings a sense of fertility and success, encouraging you to enjoy the beauty of life. Whether it's love, creativity, or material wealth, now is the time to enjoy the fruits of your labor and openly welcome all that comes your way.

The Emperor

Set the foundations for success through order and structure. This card emphasizes the importance of authority and organization in your life. You may need to establish control or make decisions that will shape your future. Show your strength and leadership to build stable foundations for what lies ahead.

The Hierophant

Connect with tradition and seek guidance in conventional values. This card may guide you toward learning and spiritual growth through known channels. You may find wisdom in ancient teachings or a mentor who can help you on your path.

The Lovers

Face important decisions and feelings that connect you with others. This card emphasizes love and harmony, but it can also indicate key decisions that will shape your future. Think about what truly matters and align yourself with your values.

The Chariot

Take control and move toward your goals with determination. This card shows you that success comes through courage and focus, even when facing challenges. Show your will and achieve what you envisioned, overcoming all obstacles in your path.

Strength

This card encourages you to find your inner strength and face challenges with courage and compassion. True strength comes from patience and control over your emotions. Show your inner strength and ability to deal with challenges calmly and kindly.

The Hermit

Take some time for introspection and seek inner guidance. This card suggests that it’s time to withdraw from everyday life and focus on personal reflection. You may find answers in silence and solitude, understanding your inner needs and desires.

Wheel of Fortune

Prepare for changes and life cycles that bring surprises. This card shows that life is constantly changing and you will face new circumstances. Learn to adapt and accept the changes that come, as they are crucial for your growth and progress.

Justice

Bring balance and fairness into your decisions and actions. This card emphasizes the need for objectivity and a just approach in all situations. Consider how your decisions affect others and strive to ensure that all aspects are in balance.

The Hanged Man

Stop the tears and change your perspective to find solutions. This card indicates the need to slow down and view things from a different angle. You may discover important insights that will help you solve problems or find new approaches.

Death

Move towards transformation and new beginnings. This card signifies the end of one period and openness to a new beginning. Let go of the past and embrace the changes that come to allow for new growth and development.

Temperance

Find balance and harmony in your life. This card encourages you to integrate different aspects of your life into a harmonious whole. Practice moderation and patience, and strive to achieve balance between different desires and needs.

The Devil

Recognize and free yourself from your attachments and addictions. This card may indicate negative influences or bad habits that keep you trapped. Working to break free from these limitations will help you find freedom and progress.

The Tower

Prepare for sudden changes and the collapse of old structures. This card often brings shocking changes that lead to necessary transformations. While it can be difficult, it offers an opportunity for a new beginning and clarity after turbulent times.

The Star

Let the light of hope and inspiration into your life. This card brings a sense of renewal and positive change after challenging times. Trust in your dreams and feel the inspiration that guides you toward a better future.

The Moon

Explore the secrets and hidden aspects of your life. This card may indicate confusion or illusions that need clarification. Pay attention to your intuition and explore deep feelings to understand what is real.

The Sun

Experience joy and success in your life. This card brings light and clarity, marking a happy period of fulfillment and satisfaction. Everything is going as it should, bringing you a sense of joy and achievement.

Judgement

This card symbolizes significant moments of discovery and evaluation, as well as the process of confronting your own actions and decisions. It represents a moment when it is time for reflection and making decisions that will shape your future. It may signify a feeling of awakening, embracing changes, and making important choices that result from past experiences. It serves as a call for self-examination and liberation from past limitations so you can move towards new opportunities and phases in life.

The World

Complete cycles and celebrate achievements. This card signifies success and fulfillment, as well as the completion of significant phases in life. It provides a sense of accomplishment and opens doors for new beginnings and opportunities.
