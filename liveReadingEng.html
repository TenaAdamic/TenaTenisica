How Live Readings Work

Live tarot readings are available on request in Maksimir Park for those who want a personal reading in a calm and natural environment. If it’s more convenient for you, you can also choose a reading via video call, allowing you to receive answers from the comfort of your own home.

1. Book Your Appointment.

2. Prepare Your Questions.
You can bring a specific question or let the tarot guide the reading.
3. Connect Live.
Choose whether you want the reading via video call, voice call, or messages.

During the reading, I will interpret the cards and provide clear answers. Feel free to ask additional questions or request clarifications during the reading.
